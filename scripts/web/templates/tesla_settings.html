Connect your Tesla account to enable vehicle controls and smart sync.

Tesla provides a free API with $10/month of credit — enough for daily use.

  1. Go to developer.tesla.com and sign in with your Tesla account
  2. Click Create Application and Access Dashboard
  3. Fill in the registration form:
    • Application Name: USB Gadget Control (do not use “Tesla” in the name)
    • Description: Personal dashcam archive and vehicle management
    • Purpose: Personal use
    • Allowed Origin: http://127.0.0.1
    • Allowed Redirect URI: http://127.0.0.1/tesla/callback
    • Scopes: Select vehicle_device_data, vehicle_location, and vehicle_cmds
  4. Once approved, copy your Client ID and Client Secret below
  5. Click Save Credentials, then Connect Tesla Account
  6. A Tesla login page will open — sign in and grant access
  7. Your browser will redirect to a page that won’t load (this is normal)
  8. Copy the full URL from your browser’s address bar and paste it back here
Your credentials are stored locally and never sent to third parties.
